Online GZIP de/compressor

This tool can compress and decompress files using the GZIP algorithm. The files are compressed/decompress right inside of your browser without transmitting the files to a server.

  • Files are compress/decompressed on your device
  • No files are transmitted
  • Size limit depends on the limitations of your browser
  • Works offline/installable
